## Platforms

### Twitter/X
- Lead with the most interesting thing
- Include a screenshot or video
- Use relevant hashtags (#buildinpublic #indiehackers #aiart)
- Keep it under 280 characters for main tweet, thread for details

### Blog/Newsletter
- More detail allowed
- Include before/after screenshots
- Link to changelog
- Personal reflection welcome

### Discord/Community
- More casual tone
- Ask for feedback
- Engage with responses

---

## What to Include

### Always
- What shipped (concrete, specific)
- What you learned (authentic, useful)

### If Available
- Numbers (honest, even if small)
- Screenshots or videos
- Customer quotes (with permission)

### Occasionally
- Behind-the-scenes challenges
- Revenue/growth updates
- Roadmap changes

---

## What to Avoid

- Vague statements ("making progress")
- Overhyping ("game-changing feature")
- Negative without context
- Competitive bashing
- Complaints without solutions

---

## Voice

- Honest about challenges
- Excited about wins (not boastful)
- Grateful to users
- Focused on value delivered
- Learning-oriented

---

## Cadence

- **Weekly:** Friday or Saturday works best
- **Consistency:** Pick a day and stick to it
- **Length:** 5-10 minutes to write, 1-2 minutes to read
